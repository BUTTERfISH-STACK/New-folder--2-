# Vellon AI Service Setup Guide

## Overview
Your Vellon CV Career Platform now supports the HuggingFace Qwen3.5-35B AI model for enhanced CV analysis, rewriting, and optimization.

## Getting Your Free HuggingFace Token

1. **Go to HuggingFace**: Visit https://huggingface.co
2. **Sign Up**: Create a free account (no credit card required)
3. **Get Token**: 
   - Click your profile icon → Settings → Access Tokens
   - Click "Create new token"
   - Name it "Vellon AI" 
   - Copy the token (starts with `hf_`)

## Activating AI in Vellon

1. Open Vellon in your browser
2. Click the 🤖 **AI** button in the navigation bar
3. Paste your HuggingFace token
4. Click "Save & Activate AI"

## AI Features

With Qwen3.5-35B AI, you get:

- ✅ **Advanced CV Analysis** - Deeper insights than local analysis
- ✅ **CV Rewriting** - AI-powered resume improvements
- ✅ **Job Description Optimization** - Tailor CV to specific jobs
- ✅ **Interview Question Generation** - Personalized practice questions
- ✅ **STAR Answer Suggestions** - Model answers based on your experience
- ✅ **Cover Letter Writing** - AI-generated cover letters
- ✅ **LinkedIn Content** - Professional headlines and summaries

## Without AI Token

The platform works without AI! It uses a local analysis engine that provides:
- ATS scoring
- Keyword analysis
- Structural feedback
- Basic suggestions

## Troubleshooting

**Token not working?**
- Make sure it starts with `hf_`
- Try creating a new token
- Check the test connection button

**Analysis taking too long?**
- AI analysis typically takes 10-30 seconds
- Check your internet connection

**Errors?**
- Check browser console (F12) for details
- Fallback to local analysis happens automatically

## Privacy

Your token is stored locally in your browser. Vellon never stores or accesses your token on any server.

## Support

For issues, check the browser console (F12) for error messages.
